What is Building the Future?

We here at Building The Future have made it our mission to accurately educate young students on the intricacies of one of the most used technologies in the world: the personal computer. Our missions statement dictates our simple goal - "Technological Education for All". We want to educate kids through hands on computer building demonstration. Through these workshops, these kids will learn how to create computers with the intent of them being donated to those in unfortunate circumstances.
